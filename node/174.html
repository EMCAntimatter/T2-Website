Videos

Streaming videos means that they will play on your computer if you click on the title.

Note: Windows Media Player is required to play streaming videos.

If the video does not play, you need to configure your protocol settings:

  • Open Windows Media Player.
  • Click on Tools then Options then Network (hit control + M if Network is not visible).
  • In the Streaming protocols area, select all the protocol check boxes (you want the protocol RTSP proxy to autodetect).

If the video says DVD, that means UNH T2 has copies of the video and we can mail you a copy to borrow for up to three weeks, free of charge. To borrow a video, send an email with your request to t2.center@unh.edu.

Note: DVDs will only play on a computer and will not play in a DVD player.

Paved Roads

This video explains how potholes are formed in our roadways.

 

Illustrates what happens below the surface of highways after 50 years of traffic. Drivers can feel the bumps and potholes, but what they don't see is how rock and soil layers supporting the roadway crumble regardless of what is done to the surface. Eventually, the highway has to be completely rebuilt and the price tag grows the longer the work is delayed. MO DOT.
