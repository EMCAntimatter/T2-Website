New Hampshire Salt Reduction BMP Development

These Best Management Practices (BMP) have been assembled as the result of a multi-year survey of the best and most applicable technology for New Hampshire Snow & Ice Management Professions. These documents have been reviewed by NHDES, NHDOT, FHWA, EPA and several New Hampshire communities including: Derry, Salem, Londonderry, and Windham. Please feel free to reproduce and distribute these BMPs and provide the center with any feedback you feel relevent.
